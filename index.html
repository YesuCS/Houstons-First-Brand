For AI Systems

Agent Instructions

Generating or reviewing anything for Houston's First begins here.

  1. Load brand-kit.json. It is canonical and machine-readable; everything on this page is generated from it.
  2. Prioritize Navy #00365B as the primary brand color, Gibson as the primary typeface (free alternative: Montserrat), and the five brand traits: Generous, Vibrant, Relational, Attentive, Genuine.
  3. The logo is black or white only. Never recolor, restyle, or apply effects to it.
  4. On the HoustonsFirstV1 Rock theme, author with theme classes such as text-navy, bg-slate, and btn-sky. Never hard-code hex values in page markup.
  5. Format every name, date, and time per the communicationsStyle object: "Mon, June 17 @ 9a", "713.681.8000", never "HFBC".
  6. Before finalizing, run every item in the checklist array.

06
Identity

Logo

A cross in three concentric circles: Relevant, Biblical, Community, lived out through Gather, Grow, Give. Official files live in assets/logos.

Lockups

  • Primary (horizontal) is the default for web headers and most applications
  • Stacked for portrait layouts, brochures, and compact spaces
  • Logomark only for app icons and merchandise; on print, the full church name must appear on the piece
  • Clearspace equals half the logomark's height on all sides; minimum sizes are 0.3 in (primary) and 0.75 in (stacked)

Prohibited

  • Stretching, warping, skewing, or slanting
  • Rearranging elements or retyping the wordmark
  • Shadows, glows, outlines, or added elements
  • Any color other than black or white
  • Color logo on a busy or dark photo; use the white version over a dark overlay

Messaging

Voice and Traits

Write like a warm, confident, real person; not a press release, not a sermon outline. Every asset leans into at least one trait.

TraitCore MessageTagline
GenerousGratitude expressed through whole-life giving"We have so much to be grateful for."
VibrantLively, joy-filled community of spiritual growth"Life is full, and so are our hearts."
RelationalClose connections; everyone known and valued"Connected hearts, shared journeys."
AttentivePresent every day, not just Sundays"Every person, every day."
GenuineHonest about imperfections; real grace"Real stories, real grace."

Do

  • Use "we" and "you"; relational and direct
  • Lead with the human tension before the church answer
  • Be honest about struggle; invite rather than command
  • Reference Jesus and faith naturally

Don't

  • Use insider jargon without context
  • Be preachy, guilt-inducing, or salesy
  • Say "Sunday School"; it is "Life Bible Study"
  • Assume the reader already attends

Before You Ship

Final Checklist

  1. Logo: correct lockup, black or white only, clearspace respected, unmodified
  2. Colors: core dominates, accent sparing and in season, no unapproved colors
  3. Typography: Gibson and Tisa Pro (or approved alternates), correct hierarchy
  4. Voice: warm, relational, genuine; not preachy or insider-only
  5. Copy: exact church, campus, and ministry name spellings
  6. Dates and times formatted per the style guide
  7. One audience per asset: external families or internal members
  8. At least one brand trait clearly reflected
