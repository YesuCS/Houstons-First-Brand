# Houston's First Brand Guidelines

Houston's First Baptist Church is a multi-campus evangelical church in the Houston, TX area. Every external-facing asset (web, social, print, digital) must reflect one unified brand identity. This document is generated from `brand-kit.json`; that file is canonical.

## Brand Foundation

**Mission:** The Great Commission; making disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19-20).

**Vision:** Relevant · Biblical · Community

**Strategy:** Gather (worship) · Grow (Life Bible Studies & discipleship) · Give (service)

**Brand Traits** shape voice, tone, and visual personality. Every asset should lean into at least one:

| Trait | Core Message | Tagline |
|---|---|---|
| Generous | Gratitude expressed through whole-life giving | "We have so much to be grateful for." |
| Vibrant | Lively, joy-filled community of spiritual growth | "Life is full, and so are our hearts." |
| Relational | Close connections; everyone known and valued | "Connected hearts, shared journeys." |
| Attentive | Present every day, not just Sundays | "Every person, every day." |
| Genuine | Honest about imperfections; real grace | "Real stories, real grace." |

## Audiences

**External (primary):** disconnected families in greater Houston; busy, often anxious, seeking authentic community. Speak to real life pressures. No insider church language. Invite, don't lecture.

**Internal:** young families (encouragement, community, resources for raising kids) and established families (vision, mentorship, deep community).

## Colors

Core palette: Slate `#1A303A` (dark backgrounds, body copy), Navy `#00365B` (headlines; primary), Sky `#40A2D8` (microtitles, CTAs, accents), Gray `#EDEEEF` (light backgrounds).

Accents used sparingly: Apricot `#FE7A36` and Kelly `#4CB963`. Each color has 75/50/25 tints; see `brand-kit.json` for exact values and PMS numbers.

Rules: never apply brand colors to the logo (black or white only); core colors dominate; accents highlight. Seasonal accents: winter = sky, spring = kelly, summer = core only, fall = apricot (mapping under Communications review; see the note in `brand-kit.json`).

On the HoustonsFirstV1 web theme, always author with theme classes (`text-navy`, `bg-slate-25`, `btn-sky`); never hard-code hex in page markup.

## Typography

Gibson Semibold for microtitles and CTAs; Gibson Light for headlines and body; Gibson Book when legibility needs a boost; Tisa Pro Regular for subheadings; Futura PT Condensed Book for dense copy in small spaces.

Free alternatives for teams without Adobe: Montserrat Extra Light (Gibson), Gentium Book Plus (Tisa Pro), Barlow Condensed Thin (Futura PT Condensed).

Hierarchy: MICROTITLE (Gibson Semibold, tracked, uppercase) → Headline (Gibson Light) → Subheading (Tisa Pro) → Body (Gibson Light/Book) → CTA (Gibson Semibold).

## Logo

The logomark is a cross centered in three concentric circles, representing the vision (Relevant · Biblical · Community) and strategy (Gather · Grow · Give). Lockups: Primary horizontal (default), Stacked (portrait/compact), Logomark only (app icons, merch; on print the full church name must appear somewhere on the piece).

Black on light backgrounds; white on dark or photo backgrounds (add a dark overlay if needed). Never stretch, rearrange, retype, apply effects, recolor, skew, place on busy backgrounds, or add elements. Clearspace = half the logomark's height on all sides. Minimums: 0.3 in primary, 0.75 in stacked.

Use the campuswide logo for general communications; campus-specific logos only for campus-targeted content (The Loop, Cypress, Downtown, Sienna, Houston's First en Español).

## Voice & Tone

Write the way a warm, confident, real person talks. Use "we" and "you". Lead with the human tension before the church answer. Be honest about struggle. Invite; don't command. Reference Jesus and faith naturally.

Avoid insider jargon without context, preachy or salesy copy, "Sunday School" (say "Life Bible Study"), and writing that assumes the reader already attends.

## Communications Style

Church name: Houston's First Baptist Church, Houston's First, HoustonsFirst.org; never HFBC, Houstons First, or lowercase domains. Contact: Firstname.Lastname@HoustonsFirst.org, 713.681.8000. Dates: "Mon, June 17"; abbreviate months except June and July; no ordinals. Times: 9a, 2:30p, Noon, 9–11a, 8a–10p; combined as "Mon, Nov 28 @ 9a". Capitalize His/Him/He for God, Bible, Word, Gospel, Church (Body of Christ); lowercase biblical, godly, church (building). Hyphen for compounds, en dash for ranges, em dash for sentence breaks. Ministry names must match the exact spellings listed in `brand-kit.json`.

## Final Checklist

Logo correct and unmodified; core colors dominate; Gibson/Tisa Pro hierarchy; warm genuine voice; exact name spellings; style-guide dates and times; one audience per asset; at least one brand trait reflected.
